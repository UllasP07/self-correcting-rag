# ACME Corp Employee Handbook (Sample)

## Paid Time Off (PTO)
Full-time employees accrue 20 days of paid vacation per year, accrued monthly at
1.667 days per month. Unused PTO of up to 5 days may be carried over into the next
calendar year; anything beyond 5 days is forfeited on December 31.

## Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Fully remote arrangements
require director-level approval and a signed remote-work agreement. Core
collaboration hours are 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM in the employee's local time zone.

## Expense Reimbursement
Business expenses under $75 do not require a receipt. Expenses of $75 or more
require an itemized receipt submitted through the Concur portal within 30 days.
Reimbursements are paid on the next payroll cycle after approval.

## Security and Data Handling
Customer PII must never be copied to personal devices. All laptops must have full
disk encryption enabled. Report a suspected security incident to
security@acme.example within one hour of discovery.

## Parental Leave
Birthing parents receive 16 weeks of paid leave. Non-birthing parents receive 8
weeks of paid leave. Leave must begin within 12 months of the birth or adoption.
